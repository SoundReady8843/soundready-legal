SoundReady Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-03

SoundReady respects your privacy.

Data We Process

  1. Account authorization data (access tokens) required to publish on connected platforms.

  3. Technical logs needed to operate and troubleshoot publishing workflows.

How We Use Data

  1. To publish content only when authorized by you.
  2. To maintain synchronization workflows between platforms.
  3. To monitor publishing status and errors.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We only transmit required data to connected platforms (YouTube, TikTok) to complete requested publishing actions.

Your Controls

You can revoke app permissions from your platform account settings at any time.

Security

We apply reasonable technical measures to protect stored credentials and publishing data.
